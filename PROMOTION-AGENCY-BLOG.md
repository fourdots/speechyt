### The Problem

Linux has always lacked quality speech-to-text solutions. Cloud services require API keys and monthly fees. Desktop apps are Mac/Windows exclusive. Developers and content creators on Linux have been stuck with slow, clunky alternatives.

### The Solution

**SpeechyT** leverages OpenAI's faster-whisper engine to deliver:
- **1-2 second transcriptions** (2-4x faster than standard Whisper)
- **100% offline operation** (complete privacy, no cloud dependencies)
- **Custom dictionary support** (auto-correct technical terms)
- **Transcription history** (never lose your work)
- **Simple mouse button trigger** (no complex UI)
